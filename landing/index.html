Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Our commitment is absolute privacy. Using client-side AES-256 encryption, your note is scrambled before it leaves your device. The decryption key is embedded in the URL fragment (#), which browsers do not send to servers. We only store unintelligible encrypted data.

Your secrets remain yours, always. Review our Technical Security Details.
